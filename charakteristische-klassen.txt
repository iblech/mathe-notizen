=== Euler

Let σ : X --> E be a generic smooth section and Z <= X its zero locus. Then Z
represents a homology class [Z] of codimension r in X, and e(E) is the
Poincaré dual of [Z].

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euler_class
